About Me

Where Medicine Meets
Machine Intelligence

I am a physician, scientist, and technology executive with over 15 years of experience building and scaling AI, informatics, and digital-health offerings across enterprise healthcare. My career bridges the worlds of clinical medicine and cutting-edge AI — giving me a rare perspective on what truly matters at the intersection of data and patient care.

I have held R&D and product leadership roles at Get Well Network, Siemens Healthineers, and Philips Research, where I have owned multi-year AI roadmaps, driven competitive displacements, and built globally distributed teams across the U.S., India, Germany, and Romania.

My work spans predictive diagnostics, NLP for clinical decision support, interoperability, EHR-derived data platforms, and AI governance frameworks — always with a commercialization mindset focused on winning the last mile of adoption.
